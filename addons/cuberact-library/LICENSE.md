## In short

cuberact-library is free to download and use, with no feature limits, the whole
time you are learning, experimenting, prototyping, jamming, and building your
game. You only buy a license at the point where you publish a **commercial**
game that uses it.

That order is on purpose. You get the full library, you can take a project all
the way to the finish line, and you decide for yourself whether it was worth it
before you ever pay a cent. If it carried your game to release, it earned the
price.
